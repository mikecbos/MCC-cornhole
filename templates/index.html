Tournament Info
How to Play:

Cornhole is played with two teams taking turns throwing bags at a raised platform with a hole in it. Points are scored when bags go through the hole (3 points) or land on the platform (1 point). First team to 21 points wins!

Tournament Rules:
  • Teams consist of two players
  • Single elimination bracket format
  • Matches are played to 21 points
  • Must win by 2 points
  • Standard ACL rules apply
